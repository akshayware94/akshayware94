### 🔬 About Me

🧬 **Computational biologist & bioinformatician** with **7+ years** of experience spanning **cancer genomics, cardiovascular disease, and multi-omics integration**.

🎓 **Ph.D. in Bioinformatics** — Manipal Academy of Higher Education *(conferred Jan 2024)*; doctoral training in **miRNA biology** and **copy-number-variation (CNV) analysis**.

💼 Currently a **Postdoctoral Researcher** at the **Institute of Cardiovascular Regeneration, Goethe-Universität Frankfurt** *(Aug 2023 – Present)*, studying **somatic mosaicism, clonal hematopoiesis (CHIP), and mosaic loss of chromosome Y (mLOY)** as cardiovascular risk biomarkers in large population cohorts (**UK Biobank, All of Us**).

🛠️ I build **scalable, reproducible NGS pipelines** (Nextflow/nf-core, Docker, Conda) on cloud infrastructure (**AWS, Google Terra, HPC/SLURM**), and work across the full analysis stack — from raw scRNA-seq / bulk RNA-seq / ChIP-seq / ATAC-seq through variant calling, pathway enrichment, and network analysis.

✍️ Author or co-author on **24 peer-reviewed publications** *(first author on 9)*.

📍 Frankfurt am Main, Germany
